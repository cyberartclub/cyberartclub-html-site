a brief history
My interest in art took off when I attended vocational graphic arts classes and ran a fandom blog during high school. After freelancing for several years, I decided to work on my long-term work, which ultimately became the Cyber Art Club. I now run around with a B.A. in Digital Media Communications as a designer and beginner game developer for web and VR.
